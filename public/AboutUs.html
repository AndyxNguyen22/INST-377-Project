About Look Books

Welcome to Look Books!
Look Books was created by two college students, Andy Nguyen and Thara Le.
The goal of Look Books is to create a book site that allows users to search for books without bias or sponsors influence.

Please feel free to contact us at anguye03@terpmail.umd.edu!
